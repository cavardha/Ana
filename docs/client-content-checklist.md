# Client Content Checklist

Ask Ana for the following before final launch:

1. Original logo PNG or SVG
2. 5 to 8 real cleaning photos
3. 2 or 3 real before/after photo pairs
4. Exact list of services
5. Exact pricing or whether prices should be hidden
6. Email address
7. Google Business Profile link
8. Instagram / Facebook links
9. Real reviews or customer testimonials
10. Booking preference: phone, WhatsApp, email, form, or calendar

## Simple message you can send to the client

Hi Ana, I prepared a professional website design for your cleaning service. To make it look more real and strong, please send your original logo, a few real cleaning photos, before/after photos, your exact services, pricing confirmation, and any customer reviews you want to show on the website.
